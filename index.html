Baby Crepe WhitePaper

Introduction

Baby Crepe is a meme-powered token built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) that combines humor, community, and sustainability. Inspired by the idea of serving “sweet reflections,” Baby Crepe rewards holders with automatic $CREPE reflections just by holding. The token embodies the lighthearted spirit of meme culture while integrating a simple yet rewarding tokenomics model.
